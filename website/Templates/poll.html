Emergency Medical Transportation

The university provides 24/7 ambulance services for emergency medical transportation. Our ambulances are fully equipped with modern medical equipment and staffed with trained medical professionals.

Emergency Contact Information

In case of a medical emergency, call our campus ambulance number immediately: +8801333199085, +8801776503469

Ambulance Service Details:

  • 24/7 availability, 365 days a year
  • Average response time: under 5 minutes for on-campus emergencies
  • Direct transport to University Medical Center or other hospitals as needed
  • Equipped with Advanced Life Support (ALS) capabilities
  • Staffed by certified Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs) and paramedics

Transportation Department Administration

About Our Transportation Department

The University Transportation Department is responsible for managing and operating all transportation services on campus, including student buses, staff shuttles, emergency vehicles, and special event transportation. Our department serves thousands of students and staff daily, committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient transportation services.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and sustainable transportation solutions that enhance mobility and accessibility for the entire university community.

Our Vision

To be a leader in innovative campus transportation services, setting the standard for collegiate transportation systems nationwide.

Our Professional Drivers

Our university transportation services employ a team of professional, experienced, and dedicated drivers who ensure the safety and comfort of our passengers. All our drivers are licensed professionals with extensive training in passenger safety, defensive driving, and emergency procedures.

Driver Qualifications & Training
  • Commercial Driver's License (CDL) with passenger endorsement
  • Minimum 3 years of professional driving experience
  • Clean driving record
  • Defensive driving certification
  • First aid and CPR certification
  • Annual safety training and performance evaluations
  • Drug and alcohol screening
Driver Responsibilities
  • Ensure passenger safety at all times
  • Maintain and follow designated routes and schedules
  • Conduct pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections
  • Report maintenance issues promptly
  • Assist passengers with special needs
  • Provide information and assistance to passengers
  • Maintain a professional demeanor and appearance
