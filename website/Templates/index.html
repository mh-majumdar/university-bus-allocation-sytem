Connecting Students, Simplifying Travel

NSTU Transit is designed to make commuting easy and efficient for all students at Noakhali Science and Technology University. With a focus on accessibility and reliability, our bus allocation system ensures that every student has a comfortable and timely journey to and from campus. By streamlining the process of bus scheduling, tracking, and seat allocation. Whether it's daily commuting or special transport needs, NSTU Transit is committed to keeping the university community connected.

Features of the NSTU Transit

Campus Commute

In this section, students can book their seat by filling up the route time of going to the university. Each student can submit only one response for the journey. Please note, the response will be accepted only within a specific time frame, ensuring accurate seat reservations for everyone.

Maijdee Commute

This section allows students to book their seat for the route from campus to Maijdee. Similar to the previous section, students can fill in their desired route time and submit only one response. The submission will be accepted within a given time window to ensure proper scheduling.

Bus Allocation

In this section, real-time statistics about bus demand for each route time are displayed. It shows the number of students per stop and the need for additional buses at each PM stop. This data helps in optimizing bus schedules to meet the student demand efficiently.
